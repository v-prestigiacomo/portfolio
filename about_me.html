I have been fascinated with technology from a very young age. Ever since I got my first desktop computer it quickly became my favorite possession. Since getting my first PC I have taken an interest in video games, graphic design and my other passion: music production.

In my mid twenties I began studying at Bournemouth University for a BSc in Music and Audio Technology. It was here that I was properly introduced to programming with exposure to C++, HTML, CSS and JavaScript. It was also at university that I became aware of the practice known as algorithmic composition - the process of utilizing algorithms to make musical decisions on behalf of the composer. This combined with my recently new found interest in computer programming opened up an entire world of creative possibilities and ever since I have had quite the reverence for programming.

Fast forward to now, I am currently studying with NetMatters on their Skills Bootcamp which covers: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, PHP and Laravel and I am looking forward to learning more as time progresses.

I am strongly motivated to continue learning as a developer as I would very much like to be able to create my own music software in the future which I imagine will take me beyond the web based languages I am currently studying.
